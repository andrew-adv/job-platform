About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our Core Experience team. In this role, you will be responsible for defining the future of how millions of businesses interact with their financial data. You'll work closely with product managers, engineers, and researchers to build seamless, high-impact experiences.

You are a systems thinker who can translate complex requirements into elegant, simple solutions. You have a passion for craftsmanship and a keen eye for detail, ensuring that every interaction feels polished and intuitive.

Responsibilities

  • check_circle Lead the design of end-to-end product experiences across web and mobile platforms.
  • check_circle Collaborate with cross-functional partners to define product strategy and roadmap.
  • check_circle Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
  • check_circle Contribute to and evolve our global design system.
  • check_circle Mentor junior and mid-level designers, fostering a culture of excellence.

Requirements

  • verified 5+ years of experience as a Product Designer in a fast-paced tech environment.
  • verified A portfolio demonstrating strong systems thinking and visual design skills.
  • verified Expert proficiency in Figma and modern prototyping tools.
  • verified Excellent communication and storytelling abilities.
  • verified Experience working with complex SaaS or fintech products is a plus.
